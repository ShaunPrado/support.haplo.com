title: Adverse events
long_title: Reporting adverse events
--

The researchers named on the ethics application form, can report adverse events related to the approved project.

The Adverse events reporting option is available on the right of the approved ethics application form. 

The appropriate user will be notified of the adverse event report. This may result in the suspension or cancellation of previously approved ethical approval. 


h3. Configuration option

This functionality is only implemented if required by the institution.
