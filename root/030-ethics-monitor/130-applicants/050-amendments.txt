title: Amendments
long_title: Application for approval of project amendments
--

Following approval of the original ethics application, the applicant can submit an application to approve amendments to the research project. 

The amendments form is available on the right of approved applications. 

Upon submission the amendments approval form is routed to the appropriate reviewers and approvers based on institutional rules.

The applicant can view the status of their amendments application on the right of the amendments record. 

The applicant is informed by email of the outcome decision.
