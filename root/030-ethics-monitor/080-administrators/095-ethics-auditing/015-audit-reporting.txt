title: Reporting
--

The auditing dashboards can be accessed under "Audits", on the Ethics activity page.

[IMAGE audit-reporting0-f.png]

h3. Audits overview

This dashboard lists all audits in the system, along with their status and other relevant information such as the exercise they belong to.

[IMAGE audit-reporting1-f.png]

h3. Audit exercises overview

This dashboard lists all audit exercises in the system, along with their status and other relevant information such as number of audits.

[IMAGE audit-reporting2-f.png]

h3. Audited items overview

This dashboard lists all the items that have been audited so far, for all types of audits.

[IMAGE audit-reporting3-f.png]
