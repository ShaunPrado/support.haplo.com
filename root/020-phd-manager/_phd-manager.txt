*Haplo PhD Manager* manages the administration and reporting of Doctoral Research programmes at Higher Education institutions. As each institution's rules, regulations and processes are different, it is delivered as a service to perform extensive customisations and provide long term support and hosting.

*We are in the process of reformatting our PDF documentation. If the documentation you need is not available here, please get in touch with your support contact.*
