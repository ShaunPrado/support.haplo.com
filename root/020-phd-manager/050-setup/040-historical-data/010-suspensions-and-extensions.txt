title: Suspensions and extensions
--

Information about suspensions and extensions for *current* PhD researchers is essential for the correct functioning of the date calculations. This information is not required for PhD researchers who have completed or withdrawn.

Please provide a file with records containing the following fields:

* username of PhD researcher (to match the username in the [node:phd-manager/setup/user-sync])
* type of record, either @suspension@ or @extension@
* start date of period
* end date of period

This file may be in any reasonable structure in JSON, XML or TSV.
